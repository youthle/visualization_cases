About me Zhouyayan Li

I got my bachelor's in Water and Hydropower Engineering in China. Back then, courses related to water resources were far more appealing to me than other courses. After graduation, I did hydrological modeling in a company. That was my first time working with large hydrological models and that experience directly led to my pursuing a higher degree in this field. I'm currently a Ph.D. student in the Civil and Environmental Engineering Department's Water and the Environment program. My research specializes in flood modeling and hydro-informatics. Outside of academics, I swim, bike, and play the guitar.
